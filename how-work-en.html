How it Works

Our system allows you to send and receive money, as well as issue bank cards with a positive balance.

Registration to the system

First you need to fill out a minimum registration form with personal details and choose a password – in minutes you will receive a verification code, which you will need to insert in order to enter the system and after a few minutes the system will be ready to use.

Start using the system

At this stage you can start receiving payments from other account owners, all you have to do is give your friend the phone number you registered with the system, and after selecting the transfer amount, and confirming the process, the money will arrive in your account. Another way to receive money is through a unique and dedicated clearing terminal that the system will provide for you – which you can embed in your online store or business site – you will receive the money directly to your account and you can withdraw it at any time to your bank account

Getting a prepaid card with a click

Our system provides a management panel for the various operations and constant monitoring. Here you can also order a prepaid card, and recharge it in a variety of ways, by making a deposit in your account, by telephone or simply transferring money to your account.

Enter the credit card receipt page, go through the steps and in a few seconds receive a prepaid credit card number, Available only for our verified users!
