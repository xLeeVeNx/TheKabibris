Prepaid Card

A prepaid card is a card which you can recharge with money and use it and pay with online or at the store.

TheKalibris saves you the most expensive thing for you, the time. You do not have to wait in the long exhausting there is no need to wait a few days for the approval to send you the card. In our company you can receive it within a few seconds after making the order. The code (CVV) on the back of the card will be sent to you by SMS and the cost of the card is minimal.

Sounds great? It’s not all! In addition, you can see the details of the transactions immediately in your account and stay updated on the status of your purchases and the amount of money remaining in your account.

From today, you do not have to pay with your personal credit card, simply order a prepaid card through TheKalibris and purchase abroad with pleasure.
